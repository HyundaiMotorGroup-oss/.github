## Hello Open Source Community 👋

Welcome to **Hyundai Motor Group**'s official open source space on GitHub.

---

## 🌐 About Us

Hyundai Motor Group is one of the world's leading automotive groups, committed to shaping the future of mobility. Our open source initiative brings together engineers, researchers, and innovators to build software that powers the next generation of vehicles and mobility solutions.

| Area | Focus |
|------|-------|
| 🏎️ **Automotive Technology** | Software-defined vehicles, embedded systems |
| 🤖 **AI & Robotics** | Autonomous driving, AI platforms, robotics frameworks |
| 🔋 **EV & Energy** | Electric vehicle platforms, battery management systems |
| 🌍 **Smart Mobility** | Urban air mobility, connected vehicle services |
